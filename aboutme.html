Since beginning her adventure as a hair stylist in 2000, Dorota Wójcik has gained considerable experience and expertise by working at boutique salons in Milan and London. Over time she has developed a unique style and sense of colouring. Dorota believes that styling is an art - she is inspired by notions of classic beauty and elegance.

As a hair stylist, Dorota combines flair and creativity with an artist's sensitivity to create original and complementary styles that accentuate her clients' individual qualities.

Dorota is at the leading edge of hair style trends and fashion. She has been trained by many notable stylists and has attended the most important academies in Europe.

Fluent in English, Italian and Polish, Dorota is constantly searching for new sources of inspiration, and finds great pleasure in collaborating with other artists, stylists and designers.
